GeoSnap – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

Introduction

Thank you for using GeoSnap: GPS Camera Geotagging ("GeoSnap"), published by Bilyx. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

GeoSnap is designed for professional geotagging and field photo documentation. We are committed to keeping your data on your device whenever possible.

Information We Collect

According to our Google Play listing, GeoSnap does not collect user data and does not share data with third parties.

Geotagging information (GPS coordinates, address, altitude, timestamps, and photo overlays) is processed locally on your device to stamp your images and generate reports.

Permissions

GeoSnap may request the following permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Camera: Used to capture photos within the app.
  • Location: Used while the app is in use to retrieve GPS coordinates and geotag photos accurately.
  • Photos & Media: Required to save captured photos and generated reports to your device.

These permissions are used only for the features described above and are not used for unrelated tracking.

Data Storage & Sharing

Your photos, location overlays, and generated PDF reports are stored on your device unless you choose to share them through your device's share sheet or other apps.

We do not sell your personal information. GeoSnap does not upload your photos or location data to Bilyx servers by default.

Children's Privacy

GeoSnap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
