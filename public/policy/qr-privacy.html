QR – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

QR is developed by Bilyx. The app uses your camera to scan QR codes and barcodes and can optionally store a local history of scans on your device.

Information We Collect

On your device

  • Camera feed, used in real time only to detect codes
  • Decoded content of scanned codes (for example, URLs or text) when shown in the app
  • Optional local history of scan results if the history feature is enabled

What we do not collect

  • No personal identity data such as name, email, or phone number, unless that information is inside a code you scan
  • No contacts, photos, or other files from your device
  • No continuous location tracking

How We Use This Information

We use the information only to:

  • Decode and display scan results to you
  • Populate your in‑app scan history (if turned on)

We do not use this information for targeted advertising or selling to third parties.

Permissions

Camera

Required to scan QR and barcodes. The camera view is used in real time and not recorded or saved by us.

Storage (optional)

May be used if you export or import scan history or screenshots (if such features exist). QR does not browse your gallery by itself.

Data Storage

  • Scan history, if enabled, is stored locally on your device.
  • Clearing app data or uninstalling QR removes stored history from your device.
  • Anonymous crash or performance data may be collected to improve the app.

Your Choices

  • Clear scan history from within the app (if the option is provided)
  • Disable camera permission to stop scanning
  • Remove all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app

Children’s Privacy

QR is not specifically targeted at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of QR after changes means you accept the updated policy.
