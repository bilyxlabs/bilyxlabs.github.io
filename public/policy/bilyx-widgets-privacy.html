Bilyx Widgets Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Bilyx Widgets Pro respects your privacy. This app is made for home screen widgets, battery widgets, Google Search-style shortcuts, and wallpapers.

Data Collection

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or user accounts in this app.

We do not operate our own servers to collect personal data from normal app use.

Local App Settings

The app saves some settings on your device, such as theme options, Material You setting, accent color, widget size settings, and widget choices.

These settings are stored locally on your device. They are not sent to us.

Battery Widgets

Battery widgets read battery information from your device, such as battery percentage and charging status.

This information is used only to display battery widgets. It stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Wallpapers

The app includes wallpapers that can be previewed, applied, or saved.

If you set a wallpaper, Android applies it to your home screen, lock screen, or both, based on your choice.

If you save a wallpaper, it is saved to your device’s Pictures folder.

We do not upload wallpapers or files from your device to our servers.

Permissions

The app may request permissions for app features, such as setting wallpapers, saving wallpapers, refreshing widgets, opening links, or notifications on supported Android versions.

You can deny optional permissions. Features that do not need those permissions will continue to work where possible.

Bilyx Widgets Pro does not currently collect location, contacts, photos, activity, or Bluetooth data for active features in this version of the app.

Third-party Apps and Websites

Some widgets are shortcuts that can open third-party apps or websites, such as Google Search, YouTube, Gmail, or browser apps.

When you tap those shortcuts, you leave Bilyx Widgets Pro. The third-party app or website may have its own privacy policy and terms.

Bilyx Widgets Pro is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google, YouTube, Gmail, or other third-party brands shown in widgets or shortcuts.

Children’s Privacy

Bilyx Widgets Pro is not made specifically for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers or analytics companies.

Data used by widgets and settings stays on your device unless you choose to open a third-party app or website.

Data Deletion

App settings and widget settings remain on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or remove widgets from your home screen.

Saved wallpapers remain on your device until you delete them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes.

If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date.
