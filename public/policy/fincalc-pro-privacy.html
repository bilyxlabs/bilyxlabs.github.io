FinCalc Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Introduction

FinCalc Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

FinCalc Pro is made for finance calculations such as loans, interest, savings, and investment planning. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Collection

We do not require you to create an account to use FinCalc Pro.

We do not use advertising SDKs or sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Calculations and values you enter are processed on your device for the app features.

Local App Data

The app may store preferences or recent calculation values locally on your device to improve your experience.

This local data is not sent to us during normal app use.

Permissions

FinCalc Pro may request only the permissions needed for app features, updates, or platform services.

You can manage app permissions in your Android device settings.

Third-party Services

The app may interact with Google Play or Android system services for installation, updates, billing, backup, or crash diagnostics. Those services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

FinCalc Pro is not made specifically for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

You can remove local app data by clearing app data in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date.
