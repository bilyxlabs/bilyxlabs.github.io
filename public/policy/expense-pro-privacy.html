ExpenseFlow Pro – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

ExpenseFlow Pro is developed by Bilyx. The app stores your expense entries so you can view totals and trends. We do not connect directly to your bank accounts or payment cards.

Information the App Stores

Expense information

  • Amounts, currencies (if supported), and dates of expenses you enter
  • Categories, tags, and notes you add to each entry
  • Optional recurring expense patterns you configure

What we do not store

  • No bank login credentials
  • No credit or debit card numbers
  • No external account balances (unless you manually type them as notes)

How We Use This Information

Your expense data is used only to:

  • Display lists of transactions by date, category, or tag
  • Generate charts and summaries for weeks and months
  • Support filters and searches inside the app

Your data is not sold or used to show third‑party ads.

Permissions and Storage

  • Your expense data is stored locally on your device (and in backups if you enable them).
  • ExpenseFlow Pro may request storage access only if you export or import backup files.
  • We may receive anonymous crash or analytics data to improve stability and features.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal financial data.
  • We may share anonymous, aggregated statistics with service providers only to improve the app.
  • We may share limited information if required to comply with law or legal processes.

Your Choices

  • Edit or delete individual expense entries at any time
  • Export data to backup formats if the feature is provided
  • Remove all data by clearing app data in system settings or uninstalling ExpenseFlow Pro

Children’s Privacy

ExpenseFlow Pro is intended for adults managing their finances and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children’s data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted here. By continuing to use ExpenseFlow Pro after changes, you accept the updated policy.
