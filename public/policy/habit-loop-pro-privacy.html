Habit Loop Pro – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Habit Loop Pro is developed by Bilyx. We respect your privacy and focus on storing your habit data safely on your device. By using Habit Loop Pro, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information the App Stores

Habit data on your device

  • Habit names, descriptions, and schedules you create
  • Completion history (when you mark a habit as done or skipped)
  • Reminder times and notification preferences

What we do not collect

  • No personal identity information such as your name, email, or phone number
  • No contacts, photos, messages, or calendar events
  • No financial or payment information

How We Use Your Information

Your habit data is used only to:

  • Display your habits, streaks, and statistics inside the app
  • Send reminders you have explicitly enabled
  • Calculate charts or summaries of your progress

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Permissions

Habit Loop Pro may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications – to remind you about habits at times you select

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Storage, Analytics, and Sharing

  • Local storage: Your habits and completion history are stored locally on your device.
  • Backups: If you use system or cloud backups, your data may be included in those backups.
  • Analytics: We may use anonymous crash or usage analytics only to improve stability and features.
  • No selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

Your Choices

  • Edit or delete habits and their history from within the app
  • Disable reminders and notifications at any time
  • Delete all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling Habit Loop Pro

Children’s Privacy

Habit Loop Pro is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child is using the app and has provided personal information, please contact us to help remove it where possible.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update this page. Your continued use of Habit Loop Pro after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
