Habit Loop – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Habit Loop is developed by Bilyx. Our goal is to keep your data private and on your device. Habit Loop stores only the information needed to track your habits and streaks.

Information the App Stores

Habit information

  • Titles of habits you create
  • Which days you mark a habit as completed

What Habit Loop does not collect

  • No personal details such as your name or email
  • No contacts, photos, files, messages, or calendar events
  • No bank or payment information

How We Use This Information

The information stored by Habit Loop is used only to:

  • Show your calendar of completed habits
  • Calculate streaks and basic completion statistics

We do not share your habit data with advertisers or sell it to third parties.

Permissions

Habit Loop uses only basic app storage and does not require sensitive permissions.

  • Notifications – only if the app offers and you enable reminders (you can disable them anytime).

Storage and Sharing

  • Your habits and completion history are saved locally on your device.
  • If your device uses backups (such as Google backups), your habit data may be included in those backups.
  • We may receive anonymous crash reports from your device or app store to improve reliability.

Your Choices

  • Edit or delete habits within the app
  • Clear app data from system settings to remove stored information
  • Uninstall Habit Loop to remove the app and its data from your device

Children’s Privacy

Habit Loop is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data about children. If you think a child has used Habit Loop and added personal information, please contact us and we will assist where possible.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how Habit Loop handles data, we will update this page. Continued use of the app after any updates means you accept the revised policy.
