Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember preferences, improve performance, and measure usage.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for core website functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand site usage and improve user experience.
  • Preference cookies: Remember selections such as theme and basic site settings.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party tools we use may place cookies (for example analytics providers). Their policies govern those cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect parts of website functionality.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when needed. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
