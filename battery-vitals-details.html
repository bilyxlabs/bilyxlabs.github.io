Take Control of Your Device’s Power with Battery Vitals! 🔋

Tired of your battery dying unexpectedly? Want to know exactly which apps are draining your power behind the scenes? Battery Vitals: Usage & Health is your all-in-one companion for monitoring your smartphone's battery health, tracking usage patterns, and keeping your device in top condition.

Designed with a focus on simplicity, privacy, and a clutter-free experience, Battery Vitals gives you the insights you need without any confusing jargon or intrusive ads.

🌟 KEY FEATURES:

📈 Real-Time Battery Health:
Discover the true capacity and wear level of your battery over time. Know exactly when it's time to consider a battery replacement and keep your phone running at peak performance.

🌡️ Live Temperature Monitoring:
Overheating kills batteries. Keep a close eye on your device's temperature in real-time. Get instant insights into thermal levels while gaming, fast-charging, or during heavy app usage.

🔍 App Drain Analysis:
Stop guessing what's killing your battery. Identify rogue, power-hungry apps with precision. See a detailed breakdown of which applications consume the most power in the background so you can restrict or uninstall them.

⚡ Charging & Discharging History:
Track your charging cycles and usage habits. View easy-to-read charts that show how fast your device is charging, how quickly it drains, and how your screen-on time affects your overall battery life.

🛡️ 100% Privacy-First & Offline:
What happens on your device, stays on your device. Battery Vitals requires zero unnecessary permissions, doesn't collect your personal data, and works entirely offline.

✨ Clutter-Free, Minimalist UI:
No confusing menus or overwhelming dashboards. Experience a clean, premium, and easy-to-navigate interface (with full Dark Mode support) designed to give you exactly what you need at a glance.
